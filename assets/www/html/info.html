About the App ?

This Application was Design and develop by Hafsat Adam Yakubu (class of 2018 NACOSS) Student of Kaduna Polytechnic. With the aim of helping student request for services at the comfort of their time and location.

What is the application all about ?

The application is the first of its kind, it's aimed at making life easy for students(in Kaduna Polytechnic, Kaduna) who are new to town and other returning students, the application contain the necessary contact information nearby services.

Why do I need the Application ?

Because the Application is a vicinity guide that can get much done at the comfort of your Home or Hostel

What is HOTLine ?

The KADPOLY HOTLine is the part of the application that contains phone numbers of various people rendering different kinds of services, and it's aimed at making life easy for students (in Kaduna Polytechnic, Kaduna) who are new to town and other returning students.
